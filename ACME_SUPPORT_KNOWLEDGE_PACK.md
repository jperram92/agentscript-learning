# Acme Customer Support Knowledge Pack

## Purpose

This document is a made-up support knowledge pack for learning Agentforce Data Library grounding. It combines FAQ, shipping policy, return policy, and support guidance into one uploadable markdown file.

## Acme Customer Support FAQ

### What does each order status mean?

- `Processing`: Your order has been received and is being prepared.
- `Packed`: Your items have been packed and are waiting to leave the warehouse.
- `Shipped`: Your order has left the warehouse and is in transit.
- `Out for Delivery`: Your order is with the carrier for final delivery today.
- `Delivered`: The carrier marked the order as delivered.
- `Cancelled`: The order was cancelled before shipment.
- `Returned`: The order was sent back and processed as a return.

### How do I check my order status?

Customers can check order status by providing the email address associated with the order and the order number. Once verified, support can provide the latest order status.

### How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 3 to 7 business days. Express shipping usually takes 1 to 2 business days. Delivery times can vary during holidays, severe weather, or carrier disruptions.

### Can I change my shipping address after placing an order?

Address changes are only possible before the order is shipped. If the order is already in `Shipped` or `Out for Delivery` status, the shipping address can't be changed through standard support.

### What if my order says delivered but I can't find it?

Customers should:

- Check the mailbox, front door, and any secure delivery locations.
- Ask household members or building staff if they accepted the delivery.
- Wait up to 24 hours in case the carrier scanned the package early.
- Contact support if the package still can't be located after 24 hours.

### How do I start a return?

Customers can contact support with the order number, the item they want to return, and the reason for the return. Eligible returns are accepted within the return window and must meet the policy requirements below.

### How long does a refund take?

Refunds are usually issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after the returned item has been received and inspected.

### What items can't be returned?

The following items are not eligible for return:

- Final sale items
- Opened hygiene products
- Gift cards
- Customized or personalized items

## Acme Shipping Policy

### Shipping Options

- `Standard Shipping`: 3 to 7 business days
- `Express Shipping`: 1 to 2 business days

### Order Processing

- Orders placed before `2:00 PM` local warehouse time are typically processed the same business day.
- Orders placed after `2:00 PM` are typically processed the next business day.
- Orders placed on weekends or public holidays are processed on the next business day.

### Tracking Information

- Tracking information is usually available within 24 hours after an order is marked as `Shipped`.
- Some carriers may show delayed scan updates during busy periods.

### Delivery Delays

Delivery delays can occur because of:

- carrier volume surges
- public holidays
- extreme weather
- incomplete or invalid shipping addresses
- customs clearance for international orders

Acme support can provide the current known status but can't guarantee carrier delivery times.

### Shipping Address Changes

- Shipping address changes are allowed only before the order is shipped.
- Once an order is shipped, support can't manually reroute every carrier shipment.

### Lost or Missing Packages

If tracking shows `Delivered` but the package is missing:

1. Confirm the shipping address on the order.
2. Check around the delivery location.
3. Ask neighbors, household members, concierge, or reception staff.
4. Wait 24 hours.
5. Contact support with the order number if still unresolved.

## Acme Returns and Refund Policy

### Return Window

Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.

### Return Conditions

To be eligible for return, items must:

- be unused
- be in original condition
- include original packaging when possible
- include any accessories or included parts

### Non-Returnable Items

The following items can't be returned:

- final sale items
- gift cards
- personalized products
- opened hygiene or personal care items

### Damaged or Incorrect Items

If an item arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect:

- the customer should contact support as soon as possible
- support may request photos
- return shipping may be waived
- replacement or refund options may be offered based on stock availability

### Refund Timing

- Returned items are inspected after arrival.
- Refunds are generally processed within 5 to 7 business days after inspection.
- Financial institutions may take additional time to post the refund.

### Return Shipping

- Customers are responsible for return shipping unless the item was damaged, defective, or incorrect.
- Original shipping fees are generally non-refundable unless required by law or due to an Acme fulfillment error.

## Acme Support Help Articles

### Help Article: How to Check Your Order Status

To check an order status, support needs:

- the email address associated with the order
- the order number

Once those details are confirmed, support can provide the latest status, such as `Processing`, `Shipped`, or `Delivered`.

### Help Article: How to Start a Return

To start a return, the customer should provide:

- order number
- item name
- reason for return

Support should confirm whether the order is within the return window and whether the item is eligible under the return policy.

### Help Article: What to Do if a Package Is Missing

If a package appears missing, the recommended steps are:

1. Verify the address on the order.
2. Check common drop-off locations.
3. Ask neighbors or household members.
4. Wait 24 hours after delivery scan.
5. Contact support if still unresolved.

### Help Article: Updating a Shipping Address

Shipping addresses can only be changed before shipment. If the order has already shipped, the customer should monitor tracking and contact the carrier where applicable.

## Support Playbook Guidance

### Recommended Tone

Support responses should be:

- concise
- clear
- polite
- specific to the customer request

### Safe Support Boundaries

Support should not:

- promise exact delivery dates not shown in tracking
- promise refunds before return inspection
- override return exclusions without policy approval
- guess order status when no order details are available

### Recommended Support Pattern

For order lookup requests:

1. Ask for the email associated with the order.
2. Ask for the order number.
3. Confirm the latest known status.
4. Offer the next relevant help step.
